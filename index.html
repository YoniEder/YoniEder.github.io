About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Weizmann Institute of Science, specializing in non-contact physiological sensing using FMCW radar. My research bridges the gap between theoretical signal processing and practical medical applications, developing innovative radar-based solutions for healthcare monitoring.

🎯 Research Focus

Advancing contactless vital signs monitoring through multi-person FMCW radar systems, from fundamental algorithm development to clinical validation in leading hospitals.

🎛️ Signal Processing

Advanced DSP techniques for FMCW radar, including sparse signal processing, multi-target detection, and robust statistical methods for physiological signal extraction.

🤖 AI & Deep Learning

Developing neural networks for radar signal processing, including RaLU-Net for precise human localization and AI-based vital signs monitoring algorithms.

📡 Radar Systems

Expertise in SISO, SIMO, and MIMO FMCW radar configurations for healthcare applications, from 24 GHz to 81 GHz frequency ranges.

🏥 Clinical Applications

Translating radar research into practical medical devices through collaborations with Sheba, Ichilov, and Schneider Children's hospitals.

Research Highlights

Multi-Person Vital Signs Monitoring via MIMO FMCW Radar

Developed a comprehensive framework for simultaneous monitoring of multiple patients using advanced MIMO radar configurations. This work enables robust vital signs extraction even in challenging clinical environments with patient movement and interference.

MIMO Radar Multi-Target Detection Clinical Validation Real-time Processing

RaLU-Net: Deep Learning for Radar Localization

Created a novel deep unfolded neural network architecture for precise human localization using radar signals. RaLU-Net achieves sub-centimeter accuracy in human positioning, enabling highly accurate vital signs monitoring.

Deep Learning Neural Networks Localization FMCW Radar

Non-invasive Nanoparticle Monitoring

Pioneered the use of mmWave radar for quantitative monitoring of iron oxide nanoparticles in biological tissues, opening new possibilities for non-invasive medical imaging and drug delivery tracking.

mmWave Radar Biomedical Imaging Nanoparticles Non-invasive Monitoring

Pediatric Pulmonary Function Testing

Developed contactless methods for pulmonary function assessment in children, eliminating the need for traditional spirometry and enabling comfortable respiratory monitoring in pediatric patients.

Pediatric Healthcare Respiratory Monitoring Contactless Sensing Clinical Application

Developed a comprehensive real-time processing pipeline for MIMO FMCW radar systems, enabling simultaneous tracking and vital signs monitoring of multiple individuals with sub-second latency.
